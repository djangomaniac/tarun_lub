OUR INFRASTRUCTURE

Spread over an area of about 25000 sq. feet that helps us in achieving our organizational goals. ABOUT US

Tarun Lubricants private limited (TLPL) is a private limited company registered under the Companies Act, 1956. The registered office of the company is situated at Mahabir Rice Complex, Midnapur, and city office at 230A, A.J.C Bose Road, Room no.- 509, Kolkata, West Bengal, India. We are in the business of manufacture and marketing of sulfonates, industrial lubricants and automotive lubricants. With a view to tap the vast potential for sulfonates and lubricants have successfully cater the same on a sustainable basis as well as cut down on the logistics cost and respond time for meeting the customer requirements in all regions.
